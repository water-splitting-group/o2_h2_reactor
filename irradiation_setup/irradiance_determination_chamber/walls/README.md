# General

This directory contains the files of the walls needed to build the irradiance determination chamber

`back_wall_v1-1` are the CAD and STL files of the back wall

`bottom_wall_v1-0` are the CAD and STL files of the bottom wall

`front_wall_v1-0` are the CAD and STL files of the front wall

`lid` are the CAD and STL files of the lid

`side_walls_v1-0` are the CAD and STL files of the side walls
